Programs at
Galgotias University

With a vision to be known globally for our value-based education, research, creativity and innovation, we at GU provide the best learning ambience for our students to explore their inner skills.

Engineering and Technology

The School of Engineering is a discipline devoted to develop students with the initial foundation, knowledge, and skills they will need to become leaders in their professional careers

Business Administration

School of Business at Galgotias University, equips young professionals with pragmatic decision-making abilities and skills to comprehend, analyze and develop business strategies.

University Polytechnic

The School of Polytechnic is aimed to facilitate our students and the faculty with exposure and platform to contemporary practices to meet various challenges in ever advancing sectors of their field

Liberal Education

School of Liberal Studies aims at inspiring its students to think out of the box and to perceive challenges as opportunities

Finance
& Commerce

The School of Finance & Commerce offers programs in the areas of Commerce and Finance at both the undergraduate as well as the post graduate level.

Basic & Applied Sciences

Applied science is the application of existing scientific knowledge to practical applications, like technology or inventions.

Architecture & Planning

School of Architecture at Galgotias University offers a unique holistic strategic framework of a balanced yet challenging curriculum of architecture degree program

Law &
Legal Studies

School of Basic and Applied Sciences equips students with technical knowledge, skill and ability; motivating them to think creatively

Medical &
Allied Sciences

The School of Medical and Allied Sciences at Galgotias University runs various programs like Pharmacy, Physiotherapy, Optometry, Medical Lab Technology and Cardiovascular Technology.

Nursing

School of Nursing aims to be globally recognized for the state-of-art value based nursing education, nursing service and multidisciplinary research to meet the health care needs of country.

Hospitality & Tourism

The School of Hospitality plans to help the industry meet the increasing demand of skilled and trained manpower by providing hospitality education to brilliant students.

Media & Communication Studies

The primary goal of the School of Media and Communication Studies (SMCS) is to nurture media and communication professionals who can leave a lasting impression in the media industry.

Education

Through School of Education we provide our students training in teaching skills that are desirous to enter in the teaching profession with perfection.

Agriculture

The School of Agriculture empowers its diverse faculties and staff to innovate, discover and push the frontiers of knowledge to address agriculture based research and education needs

Life Long
Learning

School of Lifelong Learning grooms students of GU in multi-dimensional areas thereby enabling them to effortlessly face the challenges of corporate world and also to become a better human being once they pass out from the campus.

About GU

Galgotias University is devoted to excellence in teaching, research and innovation, and to develop leaders who'll make a difference to the world. The University, which is based in Greater Noida, has an enrollment of over 15,000 students across more than 100 Undergraduate and Post Graduate programs.

The 'students-first' philosophy is a big reason why Galgotias University is consistently ranked among India's top universities. The focus of Galgotias University is on low student-to-faculty ratio that promotes plenty of personal attention and mentoring opportunities.

The record breaking placement at Galgotias this year is an apt testimony to its focus on upholding the highest academic standards right from selecting top faculty, introducing world-class pedagogical practices to personality development of the students.
